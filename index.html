CYCLISTIC BIKE SHARE ANALYSIS (SQL PROJECT)

I explored 12 months of Cyclistic bike-share data. 5.5 million trips from Nov 2024 to Oct 2025 to understand how annual members and casual riders use the service differently. Using PostgreSQL, I broke down ride frequency, duration, daily and weekly patterns, seasonal trends, and bike type preferences to uncover what influences each group’s behavior. The goal was to surface insights the marketing team could use to convert more casual riders into annual members. See the full project on GitHub linked here.
